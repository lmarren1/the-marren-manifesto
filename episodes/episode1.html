In-between Blue Skies and Dirt

Ever since I was a little kid, I have always been fascinated by aviation. I used to incessantly draw World War II airplanes and attempt to make my own out of paper and rubberband-powered balsawood planes. There was - and still is - something about flying that has always drawn me to aircraft, to the engineering that opens your life to pure magic.
