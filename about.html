What is The Marren Manifesto?

I want to master the craft of developing software. I believe dedicating myself to 10,000 hours of focused study/practice in software development will be transformative for me, and not just as a programmer.

The Marren Manifesto is my way of documenting this journey while also providing value to those around me. I see it as a way to construct a beautiful life for myself by fusing my interests into a life-long career and making some friends along the way.
